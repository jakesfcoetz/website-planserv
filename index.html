TOWN & REGIONAL PLANNING

ABOUT PLANSERV

PLANSERV is a small, hands-on Town & Regional Planning firm, offering a range of professional services to the private and public sectors. While based in Riversdale, services are available throughout the Western Cape Province.

We are dedicated to providing a superior planning service along with maintaining professional integrity and impartiality in dealing with our clients, members of the public and fellow professionals. We take time to identify, serve and satisfy the needs and expectations of our clients while keeping the balance between development and conservation of natural and historical resources. We provide a personal service no matter the scale of the project.

The establishment of a professional team is often required. We follow a multi-disciplinary approach whereby we work closely together with other professionals in the built environment, such as consulting engineers, land surveyors, architects, conveyancers and environmental specialists in order to effectively carry out a project.

THE DIRECTOR

Madie Coetzee is an ambitious and dedicated Professional Planner with more than a decade's experience in the planning field, in both the private and public sectors. She has a proven record of generating and building relationships, managing projects from concept to completion and delivering high quality work on time and within budget.

Madie graduated with distinction from the North West University (Potchefstroom campus) in 2009. She started her career with a Potchefstroom-based Town Planning firm while still being an undergraduate. During this time she finalised her professional registration with the South African Council for Planners in 2012. She established PlanServ (Pty) Ltd in 2013 and is the sole director of the company.

Her strengths include responsibility, organizational skills and professional work ethics. In her spare time, she can be found on horseback or travelling and ministering with her husband.
